Traditional Italian Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe will be the last one you’ll ever use. My Italian family’s recipe can now be yours; it's classic, simple, and absolutely delicious, as well as being a perfect freezer meal!

Ingredients

  • 4 Tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 cloves of garlic
  • 2 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley
  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 42 oz Italian tomato purée (passata) or whole tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper

Method

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Make the Bechamel Sauce
  3. Cook the noodles
  4. Assemble the Lasagna
  5. Bake the Lasagna
  6. Serve!
